Tip: protect your high-priority hours first

Tip: leave a little teal on purpose

A week planned at 100% capacity breaks the first time something runs long. Leaving a few low-priority, flexible blocks gives you somewhere to absorb the overflow, and the database keeps those gaps intact when you reload.
