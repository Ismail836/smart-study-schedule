Why a grid, not a list

Built around how a week actually looks

01
Click, don't type-scroll

Tap any hour on the grid to add a subject, set a priority, and jot a quick note — no forms buried three menus deep.

02
Color tells you what matters

High-priority sessions stand out in coral, medium in amber, low-pressure or flexible time in teal. Scan, don't read.

03
Your plan stays put

The builder saves through the server into SQLite, so closing the tab doesn't cost you the week you just planned.

Three steps

From blank week to full plan

Step 1
Open the builder

Land on a blank 7-day grid, hours 7 AM to 10 PM, ready for input.

Step 2
Block out your subjects

Click a slot, name the subject, choose a priority, save. Repeat for the week.

Step 3
Check your week-fill

A live bar shows how much of the week is planned versus still open, reflecting what is saved in the database.
