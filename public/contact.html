FAQ

Before you write in

Is my schedule data sent anywhere?

Yes, for the builder. Your schedule is saved through the server into SQLite so it can be loaded again later.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes — every page is responsive, though the full weekly grid is easiest to use on a larger screen.

Will my plan sync across devices?

It can, as long as both devices reach the same server. The schedule is stored centrally in the database.
