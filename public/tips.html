Beyond the grid

A schedule is only half the plan

Blocking out time helps you show up. What you do inside that hour decides whether it sticks. A few techniques worth building into your blocks.

TECHNIQUE
Pomodoro blocks

Work in focused 25-minute sprints with 5-minute breaks. After four sprints, take a longer 20-30 minute break.

Best for high-priority hours
TECHNIQUE
Spaced repetition

Review material at increasing intervals — a day later, a week later, a month later — instead of cramming once.

Best for memorization-heavy subjects
TECHNIQUE
Active recall

Close the notes and try to explain the concept from memory, or answer practice questions, before re-reading.

Best for exam preparation
